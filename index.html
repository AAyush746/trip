About Us

A Taste of the Himalayas in Every Bite

Located at the heart of Dhulikhel Chowk, Triple Crown Restaurant & Coffee Bar is your perfect stop on the B.P. Highway. Whether you're heading to Pokhara or exploring the ancient town of Dhulikhel, we offer a diverse culinary journey featuring Italian, Chinese, Indian, European, Asian, and traditional Nepalese cuisines.

Our property is well-maintained with a welcoming ambience, friendly staff, and surprisingly generous portions. From our unforgettable Biryani to our hearty breakfast options, every dish is crafted to fuel your mountain adventures.
